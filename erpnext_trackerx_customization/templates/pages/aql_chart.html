How to Read This Chart
  • Top Number (Green): Acceptance Number - Maximum defects allowed

Standards

ISO 2859-1 MIL-STD-105E Normal Inspection

  • Normal Inspection
  • Tightened Inspection
  • Reduced Inspection
Normal Inspection

Used for regular production when supplier quality is stable. This is the standard inspection regime for most situations.

Displayed in the main chart above.

Tightened Inspection

Applied when recent lots have been rejected or quality issues are detected. Requires smaller acceptance numbers.

When to use: After 2 out of 5 consecutive lots are rejected in normal inspection.
Reduced Inspection

Used when supplier has demonstrated consistent good quality. Allows for smaller sample sizes or higher acceptance numbers.

When to use: After 10 consecutive lots are accepted and production is stable.
