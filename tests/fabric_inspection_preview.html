Troubleshooting Steps

If you don't see this UI in your Frappe application:
  1. Clear Browser Cache: Press Ctrl+F5 (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)
  2. Restart Bench Server: Run bench restart from your frappe-bench directory
  3. Check Fabric Rolls: Ensure your document has fabric rolls in the "Fabric Rolls" table
  4. Check Console: Press F12 and look for JavaScript errors in the console
  5. Clear Frappe Cache: Run bench clear-cache
  6. Check Field Names: Verify the section "Defect Inspection by Roll" exists in your form
